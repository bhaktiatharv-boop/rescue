How can I adopt an animal? +
You can browse animals on our Adopt page, choose one, and click on "Adopt Now". Our team will contact you for verification and home check.
Is there an adoption fee? +
No adoption fee! However, a small donation is appreciated to support vaccinations, food, and medical care.
Are the animals vaccinated? +
Yes! Every animal is medically checked and vaccinated before being listed for adoption.
Can I visit the shelter? +
Yes, you can visit our partnered shelters. Visit timings and map location are provided on the Contact page.
How can I report an injured or stray animal? +
Go to the Rescue page and fill out the report form, or call our helpline available 24/7.
